Orthodontic Treatment Planning

Our system is the most comprehensive Orthodontic specific software solution for treatment planning, digital study models and CAD/CAM applications. Orthodontic treatment plan follows a number of steps with the aim to fulfil all the needs of the doctor & the patient so that the end result can be achieved within the defined time with the highest accuracy.

Planning Process

  • Complete case analysis
  • In-house treatment planning by experienced orthodontists
  • Accurate tooth movement & rotation as per the occlusion and other orthodontic requirements by using 3D orthodontic software
  • Attachments, IPR in various stages (need-based)
  • Predicted goals within the predicted time frame
  • Mid-course modification as per case Progress
  • Clear realistic goals
