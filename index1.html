What is Aligner?

32 Watts is a new generation smile correction treatment that was earlier done through traditional methods like braces. 32 Watts aligners are prepared with treatment planning by qualified senior orthodontists and are practically invisible, easy to maintain, hygienic and convenient to use as these can be removed for eating, brushing etc. Aligners are clinically proven to be as effective as previous methods for straightening and aligning teeth.

Explore More

  • Bespoke Smiles

    Having a perfect smile is a modern day parameter of beauty and aesthetics. 32 Watts clear aligner system can help you achieve the smile of your dreams. 32 Watts provides permanent alignment of your teeth in a gentle manner as compared to fixed metal braces.

  • User Experience

    32 Watts is a clear aligner which is un-noticable, gentle and removable. They are invisible and easy to use. They can be removed while eating, brushing and flossing and have to be worn 20-22 hrs per day for effective treatment. It is a virtually painless way of aligning your teeth.

  • Aesthetics

    These aligners are transparent so your friends & colleagues can not notice while you are wearing them, enabling you to be your confident self and not worrying about your looks during the treatment process.

  • Effective Treatment

    Clear aligners are scientifically proven and time tested to be as effective as braces, veneers and other orthodontic treatments at correcting a wide range of malocclusions. All 32 Watts aligners are planned and verified by qualified Orthodontists to ensure you get a perfect smile.

  • Technology Integration

    32 Watts aligners are prepared using state of art technology including 3D scanner (intraoral / desktop), world class orthodontic software and a 3D printer with high levels of precision. 32 Watts comes backed with top end dental research and world class raw material to bring to you a comfortable, precisely fitting aligner.

Aligners Vs. Braces

There are many reasons why you should consider 32 Watts Aligner instead of metal, ceramic, incognito or lingual braces.
