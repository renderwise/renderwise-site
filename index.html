What is 3D Printing?

3D Printing is the innovative technology that enables you to create a three dimensional solid object from a digital file. In order to print your desired object, you need a 3D digital model or you can use a 3D scanner to scan an object for designing the object. For designing a computer-aided design (CAD) program is used. You can easily print any model that is difficult even to imagine in short time with customized shape and size.
