Wise-Archwires

Orthodontic archwires are designed to move teeth with light continuous forces. Mechanotherapy depends on both the elastic behavior of the material and the biochemical reaction of the teeth. Selecting wire products with known qualities gives you the ability to apply force with quick, gentle and expedient results. Viva-Archwire Series has been carefully constructed to specific force levels that optimize tooth movement or groups of tooth movement at certain stages of treatment.

  • Stainless Steel
  • Nickel Titanium

Stainless Steel

Wise-Archwires SS, are heat-treated stainless steel arch wires that provide precise and predictable finishing. Designed for an anatomical fit, Viva-Archwires SS feature ovoid arch forms with unique posterior width, improved cuspid width, and a natural shape. Viva-Archwires SS are versatile and compatible with most prescriptions.

Specifications:

  • Made of 304v medical grade stainless steel
  • Ultra-high tensile austentic stainless steel wires
  • Optimum force levels
  • Highly resilient archwires
  • Resistant to permanent deformation and maintains its activation for maximum control of anchorage

Available in Round and Rectangular with natural form

Quantity: 10Pieces/Packet

Nickel Titanium

Wise-Archwires NiTi are superelastic nickel titanium (NiTi) wires that comfortably level and align teeth with easy engagement and low forces. Designed for an anatomical fit, Viva-Archwires NiTi feature ovoid arch forms with unique posterior width, improved cuspid width, and a natural shape. Viva-Archwires NiTi are versatile and compatible with most prescriptions.

Specifications:

  • Fully austenitic superelastic nickel titanium wire.
  • Superior shape memory prevents permanent distortions.
  • Delivers constant force over a long period of time.
  • Highly polished to reduce friction.
  • Excellent memory and hysteresis
  • Easier wire deflection and delivery to bracket, less stress on bonds and increased patient comfort.
  • Full shape recovery - complete unraveling and archform development by staying active to the end.

Available in round and rectangular with a natural arch form.

Quantity: 10 Pieces/Packet
