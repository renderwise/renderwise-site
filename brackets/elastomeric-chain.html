Elastomeric Chain

Our Wise-Link chain is manufactured from a thermoset polymer material which is superior to the traditional polyurethane or plastic chain. It has a more "rubber-like" consistency which provides advanced elasticity and rebound.


Specifications:

  • Latex free and hypoallergenic
  • The super polymer is odorless and tasteless.
  • It resists the stains and delivers a continuous force over a long time period.
  • Offers superior memory and durability characteristics
  • Each style of chain is precision engineered to the exact standards to ensure consistent, gentle forces for predictable tooth movement
  • Easy to ligate around the tie wings
  • Especially manufactured thin structure allows you to get under the tie wings easily without any loss of performance.

Available in Transparent and Grey color.

Quantity: 5ft/Spool
