Elastomeric Ligature

Wise-Ties ligature are made from medical grade polyurethane for superior strength and resiliency. Minimizing force degradation, they securely hold the arch wire in the bracket slot over 4 to 6 week appointment intervals, allowing for optimal torque and rotation control throughout treatment.


Specifications:

  • Latex free and hypoallergenic, so they are safe for patients.
  • Molded to be sleek and smooth, they are the perfect size for single, double, or figure-eight tying.
  • Special feature of v shape rather than o ring, which secures the wires completely towards the slot
  • The super tough polymer is odorless and tasteless
  • They maintain their original color and elasticity over a longer period of time.
  • Easy to ligate and easy to pull off.
  • High resistance to stains and discoloration

Available in Transparent, Grey and Assorted colors
