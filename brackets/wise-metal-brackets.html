Enhance Series

The Enhance Series Bracket System establishes a new standard for quality and reliability, offering all the advantages of a mini bracket with the control of a full size bracket.

The Enhance Series bracket is precisely engineered and manufactured with an emphasis on performance and accuracy.

Enhance series brackets are made of high quality stainless steel with an advanced mesh base. They are designed for maximum patient comfort and ultimate performance.

Specifications:

  • The CAD design is integrated with computerized production
  • Low profile design with rounded tie wings
  • Generous tie wing undercuts for easier legation.
  • Accurate in/out dimensions and consistent quality.
  • Compound contoured base with built in torque and angulations.
  • Small and strong ball hooks on cuspids and bicuspids
  • Highly polished surface and clean smooth slot.
  • Lower incisors are bevelled 45 degree to prevent occlusal interference.
  • Vertical scribe line for easy and accurate placement
  • The inter-wing distance has been maximized, allowing for early torque capabilities utilizing the thermal activated nickel titanium wires.
  • Superior base design, fused with a proven 80 gauge woven mesh, gives the clinician the confidence and quality demanded of today's appliances

Enhance series brackets are the economical choice for today's busy practices. If you want performance for an affordable price, this is your bracket.

Available in both ROTH and MBT prescriptions

Embrace Series

Our Embrace metal bracket series is our innovative answer to the growing demand for a true miniature appliance where strength and performance are not compromised by size.

Designed and developed utilizing state-of-the-art design and manufacturing technologies, the Embrace bracket is a true miniature twin bracket system that incorporates all the benefits of a low profile design with the superior control and ease-of-ligation typical of larger brackets.

Specifications:

  • MIM for intricate design and smooth rounded edges for maximum patient comfort
  • Compound contoured base for close fit on tooth surface, which eliminates rocking.
  • An ultra low friction slot enhances wire sliding without taxing anchorage.
  • The fully contoured base is made of 80-gauge mesh for excellent adhesion and bond strength.
  • Low profile dramatically reduces the interference of occlusion, and clinical bonding failures, without sacrificing the structural strength
  • CNC milled slot for precision and smoothness.
  • Centre scribe line for accurate bracket placement in line with the long axis of tooth.
  • Smooth and round edges for maximum patient comfort.
  • Chamfered slot for increased inter-bracket distance and enhanced arch wire performance.
  • Torque is built into the base of each bracket and anatomically contoured for accurate placement on each tooth.

Small yet sturdy, stylish yet comfortable, the Embrace bracket series offers complete control throughout treatment, while its low profile design provides greater patient acceptance through comfort and aesthetics.

Available in both ROTH and MBT prescriptions

EMBRACE SLB SERIES

Embrace SLB series is a truly unique, passive, self-ligating bracket system that performs as smoothly as it looks. The Embrace SLB bracket series provides the power and responsiveness to move teeth quickly and easily, giving you the freedom to focus on treatment, not appliances

Specifications:

  • Has a simple locking mechanism that opens with an explorer and closes with the touch of a finger.
  • The smooth clip remains passive up to .018"x.025" archwire dimension.
  • When more force is necessary, the passive system can be activated by increasing the archwire dimension.
  • Has a 4 point contact slot with rounded edges.
  • The true twin four tie wing design permits increased rotational control and the use of chain elastics.
  • The brackets have small, strong ball hooks on the cuspids
  • The contoured edges and smooth labial surfaces enhance patient comfort.
  • The unique design of the bracket base allows 30% increase in bond strength.
  • Accurate in and out dimensions provide consistent quality.
  • The compound contoured base has built in torque and angulations.
  • The lower incisors are beveled 45 degree to prevent occlusal interference.
  • There is a vertical scribe line for accurate placement and brackets are color coded for easy identification.

Available in ROTH with a 0.022 slot.
