FAQs

How much do aligners cost?

A full treatment requires approximately 15 aligner sets to be worn over one year or more. If you only need minor corrections, you may be suitable for the refinement treatment, which for sure will cost much less. For price, please consult your dentist or write to us at info@renderwise.in with your contact details, we will get in touch with you.

How long do I have to wear aligners?

It depends. A full treatment typically takes 12 – 24 months and express treatment can be as soon as 6 months. If you have extractions, it’ll be nearing 24 months because more time is needed to close the gaps.

Will teeth extraction change my face shape?

Some swear it will, but even if there’s any, it’s very subtle. You’re definitely not going to get a V-line if you have a square jaw. Infact, Lip protrusion reduces as the aligner treatment progresses, improving facial structure. If you have extraction, your front profile may appear “flatter”.

Do I need to extract my teeth for Aligner treatment?

It depends. Many times, extraction is required to make enough space for teeth movement. Without extraction, some people may also look like “having too much teeth” in the mouth after previously crooked teeth are all straightened and aligned.

How long do the extraction gaps take to close?

Only towards the end of your treatment, you will notice gaps disappearing.

How do I wear and remove aligners?

It’s easier to wear than remove aligners. When removing, use your fingers to lift the tray from the back, and slowly working to the front.

How do I clean aligners?

You should clean your aligners after every meal before putting them back on. Use your toothbrush with toothpaste to brush the inside and outside of the tray. You can soak them in Retainer Brite as well.

Can I use EMI plan to pay for aligners?

We do not offer EMI plans for treatment. But yes, you can pay in 3-4 installments within first 3 months of the whole treatment.

Is there any hidden cost for aligners?

The cost does not include X-ray, any extractions and IPR, all follow-up visits to the dentist, and retainers. The cost does not cover scaling and polishing – which is recommended to do on a quarterly basis.

After my aligners, what’s next?

You will be suggested to use retainers to wear overnight to keep your teeth in positions.
